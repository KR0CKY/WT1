Ciro Immobile

Italian professional footballer who plays as a striker for Italian club Lazio and the Italy national team.Immobile began his career at Sorrento. In 2016, he returned to Torino on loan, and was later sold to Lazio in July of that year. In his second season at Lazio, he won the Capocannoniere for a second time, with 29 goals in 33 games.
